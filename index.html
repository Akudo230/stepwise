Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our career counseling services

What types of career counseling services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive career counseling services including one-on-one sessions with expert counselors, AI-powered career guidance, personality and skills assessments, resume reviews, interview preparation, salary negotiation coaching, and career transition support.

How qualified are your career counselors?

All our career counselors are certified professionals with advanced degrees and extensive industry experience. They undergo rigorous screening and maintain ongoing professional development to provide you with the best guidance.

What are your pricing plans?

Our pricing varies by counselor expertise and session type. Individual sessions range from $110-$150 per hour. We also offer package deals and the AI counselor is available 24/7 at no additional cost for basic guidance.

How do I book a session with a counselor?

You can book a session by browsing our expert profiles, selecting your preferred counselor, choosing an available time slot from their calendar, and completing the booking process. Payment is processed securely at the time of booking.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee. You can manage your bookings through your account dashboard.
